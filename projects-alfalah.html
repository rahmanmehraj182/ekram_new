Features & Amenities

Structure & General

  • Strong R.C.C frame structure and structural design parameters based on American Concrete Institute (ACI) and BNBC code.
  • Sub-soil investigation and soil composition analysis.
  • Building planned by qualified, experienced architects and structural engineers.
  • All construction materials supervised at every stage.
  • Heavy security gateway and spacious entrance.
  • Two telephone connection points (living space & master bed).
  • Common water meter, Gas and Electricity as per Government plan.

Electrical & Substation

  • All electrical lines will be concealed.
  • Imported electrical switches, socket and circuit breaker.
  • Individual electrical meter and distribution box for each flat.
  • Each power outlet earthing connected.
  • Adequate power point/light for apartments.
  • One standby generator (Honda/equivalent) for lift and common space.
  • Per Unit: 3 Lights & 2 Fans.
  • A/c provision in 3 rooms.
  • Superior quality substation (ARK Power Ltd/Engerpac/Equivalent).

Doors, Windows & Floors

  • Imported or locally available solid wooden decorative main entrance door.
  • Internal door shutters are strong/durable veneered partex.
  • Toilet doors are Upvc and Verandahs are thui.
  • Aluminum sections in all windows with good quality 5mm glass.
  • Homogeneous tiles (16x16) in floor and wall/tiles (12x16) up to false ceiling height (RAK, Star, Cbc, Mir, X-monica, Akij, etc.).
  • Glaze tiles in all floor, kitchen, stair and bathrooms.

Lift, Water & Rooftop

  • One superior quality 8-passenger lift (Sakura, Schindler/Sanei Fuji/Giant or equivalent).
  • Decorative and attractive lobby in every floor.
  • Lift will have adequate lighting, well-finished door, cabin & intercom.
  • Submersible pump & Large underground and overhead water tank.
  • Facilities at Rooftop: Multipurpose Hall, Meeting Room, Covered Terrace, Green Space, Open Terrace, Laundry Area.
